About Charmers

Charmers started out as a small local project called Charming Bracelets. It was founded by Grace and was only available at a single location in Franklin, Tennessee — 1715 Columbia Avenue, known as MSF. At that time, customers could only buy bracelets in person.

Jameson joined the team as the marketing manager and helped grow the business's visibility. In January 2025, the name was changed to Charmers, and the business expanded online. They launched a website, a social media presence, and created the first official policy — written by Grace.

However, a month later, Grace broke the policy and was removed from the business. Jameson officially became the CEO. Charmers was officially founded on January 29, 2025, with its pre-founding date marked as December 12, 2024. The first bracelet ever made was created by Grace on January 12, 2025.

Since then, Charmers has become a real online business, offering custom handmade bracelets with fast shipping, VIP service, and support for customers across the United States and internationally.
